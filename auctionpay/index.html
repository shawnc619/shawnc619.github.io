Welcome to DEFT Auction Pay

With Macquarie Business Banking, you can now accept auction deposits from the winning bidder electronically. With no need for cheques, you can complete the process quickly and easily, and make it a little easier for prospective buyers to bid freely.

DEFT AuctionPay is an Australian first. We welcome your feedback and ideas to make it even better for you, our valued real estate clients. Please email help@macquarie.com with any queries or feedback.
