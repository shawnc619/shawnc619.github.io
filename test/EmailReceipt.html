When will this payment be received?
 
Payments made prior to the business day cut-off time will generally be received on the next business day.
 
Any payments made after this time will be processed within two business days. Business days are Monday to Friday, excluding national public holidays.
 
Business day cut off times
 
American Express and Diners Club    7.00pm (Sydney time)
Mastercard and Visa     9.30pm (Sydney time)
