DEFT reference number

This is a number containing up to 20 digits provided to you by your biller. You can usually find it on the invoice, card or letter from your biller.

Security code

This is a number printed on your card. For Visa, MasterCard and Diners cards, it is the 3 digit number printed on the back of the card. For American Express cards, it is the 4 digit number printed in small type on the front of your card.

Surcharge

This is a DEFT fee which is a percentage of the transaction amount, charged on card payments.
