Welcome to your new-look DEFT

We've improved the site and added some new features, including:

  • A DEFT wallet that makes it easy to securely store multiple bank account and card numbers
  • Notification emails when a payment fails
  • Easy management of multiple DEFT reference numbers.

You'll still have access to your payment history, scheduled payments and saved DEFT reference numbers, cards and bank accounts.
